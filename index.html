Hygienic water refers to water that is safe for consumption and free from harmful microorganisms, chemicals, or other contaminants. It is essential for maintaining good health and preventing waterborne diseases.

Unhygienic water

Unhygienic water, on the other hand, is contaminated with harmful substances that pose a risk to human health. It can be a source of various diseases like cholera, typhoid, and diarrhea.

Frequently asked questions

What are your pricing plans for both individual and bulk purchases?

Thank you for your interest! Our pricing for individual water bottles starts at $1 per liter. For bulk purchases, we offer discounts based on the quantity ordered, with prices as low as $0.80 per liter for large orders. We also provide subscription plans for regular deliveries at reduced rates. For detailed pricing, please contact our sales team.

What measures does Aqua Free take to ensure the quality and safety of the water?

Thank you for your question! At Aqua Free, we ensure water quality and safety through careful source selection, advanced multi-stage filtration, rigorous testing, and strict hygiene protocols in our bottling process. We comply with all regulatory standards and hold relevant certifications. Your safety is our priority. If you have more questions, feel free to ask.

Does your water contain any added flavors or sweeteners?

Absolutely not! Our water is pure and natural, free from any added flavors or sweeteners. We believe in providing you with the purest form of hydration.

What are the login hours?

Our login system operates around the clock. You can access your account at your convenience, anytime, day or night. Feel free to log in whenever it suits you best.

What is cost of product delivery?

Delivery costs vary based on your location and the size of your order. We strive to offer competitive rates. You can easily calculate the exact shipping fee by entering your shipping address at checkout. We appreciate your understanding!

Do I need to pay for return shipping?

Return shipping costs depend on the reason for your return. If the item is damaged or defective, we cover it. For other reasons, like a change of mind, there might be a charge. Please check our return policy or contact us for details.
